## Spider-Man: Beyond the Web

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theatrical cut">
    ### Release and runtime

    *Spider-Man: Beyond the Web* swings into theaters everywhere, clocking in at **2 hours 18 minutes**:

    * Opens with a chase across a rain-soaked Queens skyline
    * Introduces a fractured multiverse stitched back together by a rogue artifact
    * Sets up a mid-credits handoff to the next saga

    <Info>
      **Practical stunts vs digital doubles**

      The rooftop sequences blend real wirework with a [digital double](https://example.com/vfx)
      rig, where the stunt performer is captured live and rebuilt frame by frame.

      Going practical-first let the team:

      * Keep the web-swing arcs grounded in real momentum
      * Avoid the rubbery look of fully simulated traversal
      * Light the suit from real on-set sources instead of matching it later

      *When a shot demanded an impossible camera move, they fell back to a full CG takeover.*
    </Info>

    ### Soundtrack

    The score leans on a recurring four-note motif that mutates as each variant appears:

    * A low brass theme anchors the prime universe
    * Strings invert the motif for the villain's introduction

    <Info>
      Hear the breakdown of the main theme in the
      [official score commentary](https://example.com/score).
    </Info>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Director's edition">
    The extended release adds roughly 20 minutes:

    1. **A longer cold open** that reframes the artifact's origin
    2. **An alternate bridge fight** with a second variant cameo
    3. **A quieter epilogue** on the rooftop where it all began

    <Warning>
      The director's edition reorders two scenes, so timestamps from the theatrical cut will not line
      up. Use chapter markers instead of raw runtime when referencing moments.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
